Famous Tanks and Their Introduction

  • T-90M: Russia's latest version of the T-90 series, equipped with advanced armor and fire control systems.
  • M1 Abrams: The primary battle tank of the U.S., known for its heavy armor and powerful gas turbine engine.
  • Leclerc: France's main battle tank, emphasizing speed and modular armor.
  • Leopard 2A8: Germany's newest iteration of the Leopard series, combining top-tier armor and firepower.
  • Challenger 2: The UK's heavily armored and combat-proven main battle tank.
